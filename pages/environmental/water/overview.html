Water

Water is essential for the use and production of our products. With growing urban populations, increasing demand on limited water supplies, and issues with water quality facing communities around the world, it is imperative that we work together to address the challenges facing this precious resource. At P&G we are doing our part to conserve and restore water for people and nature.

Overview Of P&G Efforts

Reducing Water use during manufacturing

P&G has two overarching 2030 goals related to water use in our manufacturing facilities:

Goal
  • Protect water for people and nature in priority basins.
  • Increase water efficiency by 35% per unit of production vs. 2010
  • Source 5 billion liters of water
  • Provide 25 billion liters of clean water by 2025.

Message from Virginie Helias

At P&G, environmental sustainability is embedded in how we do business. We have a responsibility to make the world better. P&G is committed to positively impacting our homes, our communities, and our planet - especially in the areas of Packaging, Climate, Forestry, and Water. We are focused on developing innovations and products that make responsible consumption irresistible. We continue to reduce our footprint and strive for more circular approaches in our supply chain. We’re building partnerships with external organizations to combat some of the most challenging and complex issues we face today. Through this ESG Investor Portal, we’re also ensuring P&G shareowners can find data on key frameworks, including TCFD, SASB, CDP, and GRI 102.

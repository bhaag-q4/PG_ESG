Message from Virginie Helias

Much has changed in our world this year. The global pandemic has forced us to adjust our habits — spending more time at home where we’re consuming more electricity, accumulating more waste and using more water. While these changes were necessary, they presented an opportunity for all of us to reevaluate habits and routines in the home.

Studies during the pandemic show that a majority of people in the U.S. and Europe would value help from brands on ways to be more environmentally friendly at home. Consumers are demonstrating a renewed focus on maintaining a healthier and more sustainable lifestyle for the long-term — one that can positively impact our home, our community and our planet.
